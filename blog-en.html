Mountain Journal

Trails of height: stories from mountain paths

Wind, stone, and silence — short notes about mountains that teach patience and strength.

Morning on Bjelašnica

The first rays paint the peaks while the valley still wakes. The scent of pines and the cold air make every step lighter.

Trail: Observatory Time: 2h

Ascent to Prenj

Prenj is raw and honest. Stone underfoot and a view that opens only after you feel the fatigue — a mountain you have to earn.

Trail: Rujište Difficulty: moderate

Silence on Durmitor

The lakes are calm like mirrors, and the trails run through meadows and stone. The perfect place to slow down and listen to the wind.

Location: Black Lake Season: autumn

Fog on Maglić

When the clouds descend, the trail turns quiet and focused. Every step demands care, and the view rewards you only at the top.

Ascent: Prijevor Elevation: 2,386 m

Weekend on Vlašić

Gentle trails, wide meadows, and homemade tea in the mountain lodge. Ideal for a calm escape from the city and a full reset.

Trail: Babanovac Difficulty: easy

Hiking tips

  • Start early — easier trails, steadier weather.
  • Water and layered clothing are essential.
  • Always check the forecast and trail conditions.
  • Respect the mountain: leave the trail clean.

Route of the week

We chose a short panoramic walk that combines peak views and quiet pine forests.

Length: 7 km Ascent: 350 m
